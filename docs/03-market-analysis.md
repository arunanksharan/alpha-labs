## Why "Head of AI" is Achievable

1. **Most quant researchers can't build production systems** — they prototype in notebooks
2. **Most AI engineers don't understand finance** — they build generic ML pipelines
3. **The hybrid** who can do both is rare and commands premium comp
4. **Proof**: Point72 hired a 29-year-old as Head of AI. The bar is demonstrated capability, not decades of experience.

## What Demonstrates Capability

- Open-source project with real backtesting results (not just LLM opinions)
- Statistical validation of signals (IC analysis, walk-forward testing)
- Production infrastructure (data pipelines, risk management, execution)
- Published thought leadership (blog posts, meetup presentations)
- Competition track record (Numerai, WorldQuant BRAIN)

## Singapore Fintech Ecosystem Context

Singapore is Asia's fintech hub with:
- MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore) actively promoting fintech innovation
- Multiple hedge fund APAC headquarters
- Strong meetup/conference scene for AI + finance
- Tax incentives for fund management companies
- Growing demand for AI talent in financial services

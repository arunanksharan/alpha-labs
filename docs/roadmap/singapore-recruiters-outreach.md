building an open-source quant platform with multi-agent research
(github.com/arunanksharan/alpha-labs). Targeting Head of AI / Senior
Quant roles in Singapore. Would love to connect.

## The Uncomfortable Truth About Recruiters

1. **Most won't respond.** That's normal. Send 50, hear back from 10, have calls with 5, get leads from 2-3.

2. **Recruiters work for the client (the fund), not you.** They'll be helpful if your profile matches an open role. If it doesn't, they'll ghost you politely.

3. **The best recruiter conversations happen when you DON'T need the job.** Frame it as "I'm exploring the market" not "I need a job." Confidence changes the dynamic.

4. **Direct applications work.** Don't only rely on recruiters. Many $500K+ roles are filled through direct outreach to the hiring manager on LinkedIn, not through agencies.

5. **The Alpha Lab is your resume.** Every recruiter and hiring manager will click that GitHub link. Make sure it's impressive. It is.

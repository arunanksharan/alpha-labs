## Papers Behind Paywalls (How to Access)

### "Implementing AI Foundation Models in Asset Management" — López de Prado & Fabozzi (2025)

This is the most directly relevant paper for a Head of AI role. It's behind the PM Research paywall.

**5 ways to access it:**

1. **PM Research subscription**: [pm-research.com/content/iijpormgmt/52/2/11](https://www.pm-research.com/content/iijpormgmt/52/2/11)
   - Individual article: ~$40
   - Journal subscription: ~$400/year

2. **Ask López de Prado directly on LinkedIn**:
   - He's active: [linkedin.com/in/lopezdeprado](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lopezdeprado/)
   - Message: "Dr. López de Prado, I'm building an AI-native quant research platform in Singapore (github.com/arunanksharan/alpha-labs) and would love to read your paper on implementing AI foundation models in asset management. Would you be willing to share a preprint?"
   - He has shared papers before when researchers ask politely.

3. **CFA Institute access**:
   - CFA members may have access through the Research Foundation
   - Recent related paper (free): [AI in Asset Management: Tools, Applications, and Frontiers](https://rpc.cfainstitute.org/research/foundation/2025/ai-in-asset-management-book)

4. **Singapore university library**:
   - NUS, NTU, SMU libraries have PM Research access
   - Walk into any university library with your ID
   - Or if you enroll in CQF/MFE, you get library access

5. **ResearchGate or Google Scholar**:
   - Search: "Implementing AI Foundation Models Asset Management López de Prado"
   - Authors sometimes upload preprints to ResearchGate
   - [Google Scholar link](https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=%22Implementing+AI+Foundation+Models+in+Asset+Management%22)
